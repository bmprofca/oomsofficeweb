# Overview

## Product

- **Name:** OOMS v4 Client (Office Operations Management System)
- **Type:** React SPA
- **Domain:** CA/accounting/tax workflow management

## Core Scope

- Client/staff/task lifecycle
- Billing and voucher flows
- Ledger and finance operations
- Office assistance tools
- Settings and branch-level configuration

## Primary Goal

Provide a single business UI for operations-heavy accounting teams with consistent admin-style interactions across modules.
